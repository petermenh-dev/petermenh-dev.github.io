# Portfolio Strategy Guide

## Positioning (Most Important)

You're not just:
> "a developer with a portfolio"

You're closer to:
> **Senior developer transitioning into product-minded / leadership roles with a focus on systems thinking**

Lean into that.

---

## What Your Portfolio Should Feel Like

Not a gallery. More like:

- "This person thinks clearly"
- "This person organizes chaos"
- "This person could lead something"

If someone skims for 20 seconds, they should get that.

---

## Structure (Keep It Tight)

### 1. Landing (Your Hook)

**Headline:**
> I build systems that scale—from code to teams.

**Subtext:**
- What you do
- What you're aiming for (IC → Lead / PM direction)

Optional:
- “Current Focus” section to feel active

---

### 2. Design System (Your Secret Weapon)

Turn this into a **case study**, not a dump.

Include:
- Why you made it (problem thinking)
- Principles (consistency, scalability, usability)
- Examples (components, tokens, patterns)
- Tradeoffs (this is huge for senior-level perception)

Signals:
- Systems thinking
- Product mindset
- Leadership potential

---

### 3. Selected Work (Max 3 Projects)

For each project:

- Problem
- Your role
- Key decision you made
- Outcome (even if rough)

Goal:
> Show how you think, not just what you built

---

### 4. Resume

- Viewable
- Downloadable
- Skimmable

Add a highlight strip:
- Led X
- Improved Y
- Reduced Z

---

### 5. Personality (Light Touch)

Skip long essays.

Instead:

**How I Work**
- I value clarity over cleverness
- I like systems over one-offs
- I think in tradeoffs

---

## Creative Ideas (That Actually Stand Out)

### 1. Decision Log

Show:
- Real decisions
- Why you made them

Great for senior-level positioning.

---

### 2. Interactive Design System

Even simple interactivity helps:
- Theme toggles
- Component states
- Spacing scale

Shows system thinking.

---

### 3. "If I Had More Time..."

For each project:
- What you'd improve
- What you'd cut

Signals maturity.

---

### 4. Vibe-Coding Angle (Use Carefully)

Avoid:
> "AI built this"

Instead:
> I use AI tools to accelerate iteration, not replace thinking.

---

## Marketing Yourself

### Your Angle

You're targeting:
- Teams that need clarity
- Teams that are messy
- Teams that are scaling

Message:
> I reduce chaos.

---

## Where to Use This

### 1. LinkedIn

Post:
- Design system snippets
- Decisions you made
- Before/after thinking

Avoid:
> "Check out my portfolio"

Instead:
> "Here’s how I standardized spacing across a system..."

---

### 2. Resume Link Strategy

Instead of:
> Portfolio

Use:
> Portfolio (focused on system design + decision-making)

---

### 3. Quiet Networking Move

Say:
> I’ve been working on a design system and documenting decisions—it’s on my site if you're curious.

Low pressure, high signal.

---

## Reality Check

Most portfolios fail because:
- Too many projects
- No narrative
- No decision-making shown

Fix those, and you're ahead.
